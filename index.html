About Me

Growing up in a household shaped by law enforcement and military service, I was exposed early to the responsibility of protecting others. My father's work as a Pennsylvania State Police officer, combined with my family's deep military background, instilled in me a strong sense of discipline, vigilance, and service.

As my passion for technology developed, I recognized a clear intersection between those values and the digital world. Cybersecurity emerged as the ideal path — the same mission of protection, on a different battlefield.

"If the shortage of cybersecurity professionals were the case in any other profession, it would be a nationwide crisis."
— Dr. Waleed Farag, Indiana University of Pennsylvania

That quote has stuck with me since sophomore year. The talent gap in this field is real, and I want to be part of closing it.
